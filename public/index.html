Everything you need

Built for real trips,
not spreadsheets.

Every feature is designed around how groups actually split things: messy, unequal and across different currencies.

handshake
Smart Settlements
Splittr minimises the number of transactions needed to clear all debts. The algorithm finds the optimal path so no one pays more times than necessary.
cloud_sync
Google Drive Backup
Your data is stored in a hidden app folder inside your own Google Drive. No Splittr servers hold your expenses; private by design.
receipt_long
Flexible Split Modes
Split equally or set custom amounts per person. Perfect for when someone only ordered a salad.
currency_exchange
10+ Currencies
INR, USD, EUR, GBP, JPY, CAD, AUD, SGD, CHF and MXN. Each group has its own independent currency setting.
picture_as_pdf
PDF & CSV Export
Generate a full expense report as a PDF or export a clean CSV for your own records and spreadsheets.
bar_chart
Spending Stats
Category breakdowns, per-member summaries and a full timeline of every expense logged in the group.
cloud_off
Works Offline
All calculations run in the browser. You can log expenses and view settlements without an internet connection.
share
Share Summary Card
Generate a shareable image or text snapshot of the group balance and send it to anyone in seconds.
edit_calendar
Join Date Tracking
Members can have different join dates so late additions only share expenses from when they joined.
Simple flow

Up and running
in two minutes.

1
Create a group
Name your trip or household, pick a currency and add everyone who is splitting costs together.
2
Log expenses
Add expenses as they happen: who paid, who was involved, the amount and an optional category.
3
Settle up
Splittr shows the minimum transactions needed. Record each payment as it happens and mark it done.
