Everything you need

Built for real trips,
not spreadsheets.

Every feature designed around how groups actually split things — messy, unequal, and across different currencies.

handshake
Smart Settlements
Splittr minimises the number of transactions needed to settle all debts. No one pays more times than they have to — the algorithm figures out the optimal path automatically.
cloud_sync
Google Drive Backup
Your data lives in a hidden app folder in your own Drive. No Splittr servers, no data harvesting — private by architecture.
receipt_long
Flexible Split Modes
Split equally, or set custom amounts per person. Perfect for when one person only had a salad.
currency_exchange
10+ Currencies
INR, USD, EUR, GBP, JPY, CAD, AUD and more. Each group has its own independent currency setting.
picture_as_pdf
PDF & CSV Export
Generate a full expense report as PDF, or export CSV for your own records and spreadsheets.
bar_chart
Spending Stats
Per-group stats showing who spent what, category breakdowns, and a full timeline of expenses.
Simple flow

Up and running
in two minutes.

1
Create a group
Name your trip or flatmate group, pick a currency, and add everyone who's splitting costs.
2
Log expenses
Add expenses as they happen — who paid, who was involved, and optionally a category.
3
Settle up
Splittr calculates the minimum transfers needed. Record payments as they happen and mark done.
