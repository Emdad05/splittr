FAQ

Common questions

Can't find what you're looking for? The app is fully self-contained — just open it and explore.

Is Splittr free?add
Yes — completely free, with no hidden fees, no premium tier, and no ads. Splittr is a passion project built to make group finances painless.
Do I need to sign in?add
No. You can use Splittr as a guest — your data is stored locally in your browser. Signing in with Google adds automatic cloud backup to your private Drive folder, so you never lose data if you clear your browser or switch devices.
Where is my data stored?add
When you sign in, your data is stored in a hidden "appdata" folder in your own Google Drive — only you can see it. Splittr never has access to your other Drive files. In guest mode, everything stays in your browser's localStorage.
Can other group members see my data?add
No. Splittr is a single-user app — your groups and expenses are stored privately to you. It's designed for one person to track on behalf of the group, then share the final PDF report or simply read out who owes what.
What happens if I used it as a guest and then sign in?add
When you sign in after using guest mode, Splittr detects your existing local data and offers to sync it to your Drive, so nothing is lost. Your groups migrate seamlessly.
How does the settlement algorithm work?add
Splittr calculates each member's net balance (total paid minus their fair share). It then finds the minimum number of bank transfers to zero everyone out — meaning fewer transactions for your group to execute.
Can I export or back up my data?add
Yes. Every group has an "Export PDF" option that generates a full printable report, and a "Backup JSON" option that downloads your raw data. You can also restore from a JSON backup at any time from the group actions menu.
Is Splittr available on mobile?add
Yes — Splittr is a Progressive Web App (PWA) and is fully optimised for mobile browsers. You can add it to your home screen on iOS and Android for an app-like experience without installing anything from a store.
Which currencies are supported?add
USD, EUR, GBP, INR, JPY, CAD, AUD, SGD, CHF, and MXN — with proper locale-aware formatting for each. Each group can have its own currency.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

We believe privacy is a right, not a feature. Here is exactly what we do and don't do with your data.

1. Who we are

Splittr ("the app", "we", "us") is an independent, personally operated web application. It is not affiliated with any company. The operator can be contacted via the app's GitHub repository or by email at hello@splittr.app.

2. What data we collect

When you sign in with Google, we receive and store only the minimum information needed to operate the app:

  • Your Google account ID (a numeric identifier)
  • Your display name and email address
  • Your profile picture URL
  • OAuth tokens required to read and write to your Google Drive appdata folder

This information is stored in a secure, signed, HTTP-only session cookie on your device. Tokens are never transmitted to any third-party service.

3. Your expense data

All group and expense data you create is stored exclusively in a hidden "appdata" folder within your own Google Drive account. This folder is:

  • Invisible to you in the normal Drive interface
  • Invisible to all other users and apps
  • Readable only by Splittr (when you are signed in) and by you via the Google Drive API
  • Deleted automatically if you revoke Splittr's access or disconnect the app from your Google account

We do not store your expense data on our own servers. Our servers act only as a proxy to transfer data between your browser and your Drive.

4. Google Drive scope

Splittr requests only the drive.appdata scope — the narrowest possible Drive permission. This means we can only access a private application folder; we cannot see, read, or modify any other files in your Google Drive.

5. Guest mode

If you use Splittr without signing in ("guest mode"), no data leaves your device. Everything is stored in your browser's localStorage. We have no visibility into this data whatsoever.

6. Cookies and local storage

  • splittr_sess — a signed HTTP-only session cookie containing your session data. Expires after 30 days.
  • splittr_has_session — a non-sensitive indicator cookie used by the frontend to detect an active session. Expires after 30 days.
  • split-v4 — localStorage key containing your group data (browser only, not transmitted).
  • splittr-theme — localStorage key for your dark/light mode preference.

7. Third-party services

Splittr uses Google OAuth 2.0 for authentication and the Google Drive API for cloud storage. Google's privacy practices are governed by the Google Privacy Policy. We do not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

8. Data retention and deletion

Your session data is deleted when you sign out or when the session cookie expires (30 days). Your expense data persists in your Google Drive until you delete it — either by deleting individual groups in the app, or by revoking Splittr's access from your Google Account permissions.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy when features change. When we do, the "Last updated" date at the top will change. Continued use of Splittr after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

Questions about your privacy? Email us at hello@splittr.app. We take privacy seriously and will respond within 72 hours.
