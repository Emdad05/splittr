infoOverview

Privacy-first design: Splittr is built so your expense data never touches our servers. When you sign in with Google, your groups and expenses are stored directly in your own Google Drive. We only hold a temporary session token to keep you logged in.

This Privacy Policy describes what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using Splittr you agree to the practices described here.

data_usageData we collect

We collect the minimum information necessary to operate the service:

  • Google profile information — Your name, email address and profile picture URL, used only to display your identity within the app.
  • Google Drive access token — A scoped OAuth token stored server-side in your encrypted session. Never logged or shared.
  • Session data — An encrypted cookie to keep you authenticated. Contains no expense data.
  • Server logs — Standard HTTP logs (IP, browser type, pages visited, timestamps) retained up to 30 days.

We do not collect: payment details, location data, or any expense content such as amounts, descriptions or member names.

manage_accountsHow we use your data

Your data is used solely to operate the service: authenticate you, display your name and avatar, read and write your Drive data on your behalf, and diagnose errors using anonymised logs.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling or any commercial purpose beyond operating Splittr.

cloud_syncGoogle Drive storage

When you sign in, Splittr requests permission to access a dedicated app folder in your Google Drive. This folder:

  • Is hidden from your regular Drive file list — it does not appear in "My Drive".
  • Is accessible only to Splittr and you.
  • Can be deleted by revoking Splittr's access in your Google Account permissions.

Your data, your Drive. Splittr's servers are only a relay — we never retain expense data server-side. If you delete your Google account or revoke access, your expense data is gone.

cookieCookies and sessions

We use two cookies:

  • splittr_has_session — A session indicator set when you sign in. Expires on sign-out or after 30 days.
  • splittr_guest — Set when you choose guest mode. Contains no personal data. Expires after 30 days.

We do not use advertising, analytics or third-party tracking cookies.

shareThird parties

  • Google OAuth 2.0 and Drive API — For sign-in and storage. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Vercel — Our hosting provider. Subject to Vercel's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts — For typography, loaded from Google's CDN.

We do not sell, rent or share your personal information with any other third parties.

delete_foreverYour rights and deletion

  • Access — View all your data directly in your Google Drive app folder at any time.
  • Deletion — Revoke Splittr's access in Google Account settings to immediately delete all app folder data.
  • Export — Use "Backup JSON" or "Export PDF/CSV" within any group to download your data.
  • Portability — Your data is stored as JSON in your own Drive, giving you full control.

securitySecurity

  • All traffic is encrypted in transit via HTTPS/TLS.
  • Session tokens are encrypted and never stored in plain text.
  • OAuth tokens are scoped to minimum required permissions (drive.appdata only).
  • We do not log expense content or OAuth access tokens.

No system is 100% secure. While we implement reasonable safeguards, we cannot guarantee absolute security. Please report vulnerabilities responsibly.

child_careChildren

Splittr is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

updateChanges to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of Splittr after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

mailContact

For privacy questions, data deletion requests or to report a security issue, please open an issue on the project repository or reach out through the app's support channel. We aim to respond within 5 business days.
