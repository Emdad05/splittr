infoOverview

Privacy-first design: Splittr is built so your expense data never touches our servers. When you sign in with Google, your groups and expenses are stored directly in your own Google Drive. We only hold a temporary session token to keep you logged in.

Splittr provides an expense-splitting service accessible at this domain. This Privacy Policy describes what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

By using Splittr you agree to the practices described here. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the service.

data_usageData we collect

We collect the minimum information necessary to operate the service:

  • Google profile information - When you sign in via Google OAuth, we receive your name, email address and profile picture URL. This is used only to display your identity within the app.
  • Google Drive access token - A scoped OAuth token that allows Splittr to read and write to a private app folder in your Google Drive. This token is stored server-side only as part of your encrypted session and is never logged or shared.
  • Session data - An encrypted session cookie to keep you authenticated between visits. It contains no expense data.
  • Server logs - Standard HTTP request logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited, timestamps) retained for up to 30 days for security and debugging.

We do not collect: payment card details, national ID, location data or any expense content such as amounts, descriptions or member names. All expense data is stored in your own Google Drive.

manage_accountsHow we use your data

Your data is used solely to operate the service:

  • Authenticate you via Google OAuth and maintain your login session.
  • Display your name and avatar in the app interface.
  • Read and write expense data to your own Google Drive app folder on your behalf.
  • Diagnose errors and maintain service reliability using anonymised log data.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling or any commercial purpose beyond operating Splittr.

cloud_syncGoogle Drive storage

When you sign in, Splittr requests permission to access a dedicated app folder in your Google Drive. This folder:

  • Is hidden from your regular Drive file list; it does not appear in "My Drive".
  • Is accessible only to Splittr and you. No other app or Google service can see it.
  • Can be deleted at any time by revoking Splittr's access in your Google Account permissions, which deletes all stored data.

Your data, your Drive. Splittr's servers are only a relay: we read and write on your behalf but never retain expense data server-side. If you delete your Google account or revoke our access, your expense data is gone.

If you use Splittr as a guest (without signing in), all data is stored locally in your browser's localStorage only. This data is not synced and will be lost if you clear your browser storage.

cookieCookies and sessions

We use the following cookies:

  • splittr_has_session - A session indicator set when you sign in. Expires when you sign out or after 30 days.
  • splittr_guest - Set when you choose guest mode. Contains no personal data. Expires after 30 days.

We do not use advertising cookies, analytics cookies or any third-party tracking cookies.

shareThird parties

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google OAuth 2.0 and Google Drive API - For sign-in and Drive storage. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Vercel - Our hosting provider. Subject to Vercel's Privacy Policy. Server logs may be stored by Vercel.
  • Google Fonts - For typography. Font files are loaded from Google's CDN.

We do not sell, rent or share your personal information with any other third parties.

delete_foreverYour rights and deletion

  • Access - You can view all your expense data directly in your Google Drive app folder at any time.
  • Deletion - Revoke Splittr's access in Google Account settings to immediately delete all app folder data. Alternatively, contact us and we will confirm deletion of any server-held session data within 30 days.
  • Export - Use the "Backup JSON" or "Export PDF/CSV" features within any group to download your data at any time.
  • Portability - Your data is stored as JSON files in your own Drive, giving you full control and portability.

securitySecurity

  • All traffic is encrypted in transit via HTTPS/TLS.
  • Session tokens are encrypted and never stored in plain text.
  • OAuth tokens are scoped to the minimum required permissions (Drive app folder only, drive.appdata scope).
  • We do not log expense content or OAuth access tokens.

No system is 100% secure. While we implement reasonable safeguards, we cannot guarantee absolute security. If you discover a vulnerability, please report it responsibly via the contact address below.

child_careChildren

Splittr is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

updateChanges to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of Splittr after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

mailContact

For privacy questions, data deletion requests or to report a security issue, please open an issue on the project repository or reach out through the app's support channel. We aim to respond within 5 business days.
